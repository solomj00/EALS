Advocating for safer streets, better transit, and vibrant communities for people walking, biking, and using public transportation.

Walkable Communities

Safer crosswalks, slower traffic, and pedestrian-first design to make every neighborhood walkable and livable.

Better Biking

Protected bike lanes, secure parking, and education programs to make biking a safe and easy choice for everyone.

Reliable Transit

Frequent, fast, and affordable transit options that connect people to jobs, education, and opportunity.
